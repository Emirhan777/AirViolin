FAQ

Does the app record audio?
No. Air Violin does not record or store audio.

Does the app collect data?
No personal data is collected. Motion processing happens on-device.

Do I need extra hardware?
No. Just your iPhone and its sensors.

Privacy

Air Violin uses motion sensors (device orientation and movement) to control sound. All processing happens locally on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share personal information.
