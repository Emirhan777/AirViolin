Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Air Violin handles information when you use the app. Your privacy is important, and the app is designed to work without collecting personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

Air Violin does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, the app does not collect:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Audio recordings
  • Usage analytics or tracking identifiers
  • Device identifiers for advertising or profiling

Motion Sensor Usage

Air Violin uses your device’s built-in motion sensors (such as orientation and movement) to control sound in real time. This data is processed locally on your device only and is never stored or transmitted.

Audio

The app generates sound based on your movements. No audio is recorded, saved, or sent outside your device.

Third-Party Services

Air Violin does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

Air Violin does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Because the app does not collect data at all, it is safe to use for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If the app’s privacy practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any changes will be posted on this page.
